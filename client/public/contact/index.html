What to write about

Bug reports

Found something broken? Please send an email with:

  • What you were doing when the bug occurred (e.g., "creating a room", "during the seeking phase").
  • What you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
  • Your browser, operating system, and whether you were on mobile or desktop.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if you can capture one.
  • Your room code, if it's a multiplayer-state issue (optional — helps us reproduce).

The more details you include, the faster we can fix it. Email adarshchauhan13@gmail.com with the subject line "Bug Report".

Feature requests and feedback

Have an idea for a new game mode, a tweak to the scoring, a UI improvement, or just a vibe you want us to capture? We genuinely love these emails. There is no formal process — write us a paragraph or two explaining what you want and why, and we will read it carefully.

Email adarshchauhan13@gmail.com with the subject line "Feedback".

Privacy and data requests

For privacy questions, data access requests, or anything covered in our Privacy Policy, write us at adarshchauhan13@gmail.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject. We will reply within 30 days as required by applicable law.

Legal and copyright

If you believe content on GeoHiders infringes your copyright or other rights, send a takedown notice to adarshchauhan13@gmail.com with "Legal Notice" in the subject. Include enough detail to identify the work in question, your contact details, and a statement that you are authorized to act on behalf of the rights holder.

Press, partnerships, and advertising

Writing an article about geography games or want to partner on something? Email adarshchauhan13@gmail.com with "Partnership" in the subject. Tell us who you are, what you have in mind, and any relevant links.

Response time

GeoHiders is a side project, not a company with a dedicated support team. We do our best to read everything within a few days and respond to most messages within a week. If your message is urgent (e.g., a security issue), please mark the subject line accordingly and we will prioritize it.

What not to email about

To save time, please don't email us about:

  • "Add me as an admin" — GeoHiders has no admin tier; the host of a room is the admin of that room.
  • SEO services, link-building, or unsolicited marketing — these go straight to trash.
  • Buying or selling display ad slots — all ads are managed by Google AdSense and we don't sell direct inventory.
